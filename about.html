Our Purpose

Recently, we have noticed the severity of the problem Alzheimer's disease poses to society, as well as the lack of awareness. We have decided to start tackling this problem by influencing those near us, in our school and the local communities. We hope that the information we provide is useful for preventing the disease, and responding to Alzheimer's diagnosis.
