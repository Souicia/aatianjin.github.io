Actions for Alzheimer's Disease

Prevention

Prevention – Cardiovascular Health

CDC studies have revealed that people carrying four or more risk factors related to cardiovascular disease have six times the probability of developing Alzheimer's disease.

Prevention – Sleep

Sleep has two functions in preventing Alzheimer's:

  1. Sleep has the function of cleaning the material (amyloid proteins) causing Alzheimer's Disease
  2. Sleep improves cardiovascular health.

Prevention – Learning

Learning new activities can help create new connections within the brain, which can make up for the destroyed brain functions because of Alzheimer's disease.

Early Detection

  • Many drugs / drug trials are only effective on early-stage dementia
  • More time to produce a care plan

10 Signs of Dementia

From Alzheimer's Association

1. Memory loss that disrupts daily life

2. Challenges in planning or solving problems

3. Difficulty completing familiar tasks

4. Confusion with time / place

5. Trouble understanding visual images and spatial relationships

6. New problems with words in speaking or writing

7. Misplacing things and losing the ability to retrace steps

8. Decreased or poor judgement

9. Withdrawal from work or social activities

10. Changes in mood and personality
